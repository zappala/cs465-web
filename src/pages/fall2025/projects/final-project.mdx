# Final Project

Spend three hours learning something new using the material on
[pwn.college](https://pwn.college).

Choose from the following options:

- [Reverse Engineering](https://pwn.college/intro-to-cybersecurity/reverse-engineering/)
  -- you will learn how to reverse engineer a binary
- [Asymmetric Cryptography](https://pwn.college/intro-to-cybersecurity/cryptography/)
  -- you will learn to build your own working versions of algorithms
- [Return Oriented Programming](https://pwn.college/program-security/return-oriented-programming/)
  -- you will learn how to use ROP to exploit a program

## Deliverables

Turn in:

- Screenshots of your progress after 3 hours
- One or two paragraphs explaining what you have learned (take an additional 10
  to 15 minutes to write this)
